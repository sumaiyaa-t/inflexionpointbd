2. END-TO-END COMPREHENSIVE DIGITAL PLATFORM FOR RISK MITIGATION OF LIVESTOCK

Currently, risk mitigation for the cattle farmers for their bovines involves marking methods like Ear Tagging, Branding, or Tattooing which can easily be replicated. Recent attempts at digitalization used RFID or NFC tags or Bolus which are expensive, impractical, and at risk of being tampered with. No more working with vulnerable Claim Management Processes or haggling with higher premiums due to the absence of a historical database on cattle deaths, our robust online platforms have solutions for every step of the Insurance Journey.

It is a livestock risk mitigation digital platform that provides financial security using a farmer-centric, technology-enabled ecosystem. We have facilitated the development of customer-centric livestock insurance products via InsureCow. It is a Cloud hosted technology platform for Insurance Policy Registration and Monitoring. Cattle Recognition Facility through Machine Learning is used to work with Cattle Muzzle Print during swift Claim Raising Solutions. Using our platform, you will be able to transparently track Policies in real-time. Not just this, you will also have access to relevant information through a real-time dashboard and managerial support.

  • Cattle Registration Services
  • Cattle identification service through muzzle printometry
  • Platform & API Services
  • Data Management and Visualization Services
  • Cloud Hosting
  • Insurance Product Designing & Underwriting Services
  • Claim Management
  • Payment Gateway for All Stakeholders
